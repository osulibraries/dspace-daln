WSU CORE

WSU CORE (Campus Online Repository) is Wright State's institutional repository. It is a part of OhioLINK's newly developed Ohio Digital Resources Commons (DRC). This is the beta version of the site. We are currently working on developing a new look and are adding new content frequently.

Ohio Digital Resource Commons

The Digital Resources Commons (DRC) is network of centrally hosted repositories that enables institutions to save, discover and share, the instructional, research, historic and creative materials produced by the University System of Ohio and Ohio's liberal arts colleges. The DRC features local branding, remote submission and administration, and federated searching.
